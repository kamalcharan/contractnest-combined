## 2. Locked taxonomy & data model decisions

- Every chargeable non-spare-part is a **Service** (facilities call spares "consumables"). Services/spares are billed **as performed** (`/visit`, `/unit`); a "Service Contract" (PM/AMC/CMC) is a **template/contract-layer** bundle with duration. Cadence is DATA (`config.serviceCycles`), never part of a service name (no frequency words in names).
- Block grouping key in mapper: `cycle.catalog_name || checkpoint.service_name`; no key = unsellable, skipped.
- Multi-currency: `m_kt_prices` (UNIQUE entity_type, entity_id, geo); INR is default/home (INR-pin slot policy); UI shows currency pills, INR first.
- Descriptions: stored ONCE per service in `m_kt_service_definitions`; flow into `m_cat_blocks.description` (services AND spares).
- **NEW today — `m_checkpoint_variant_map`** is generated by the checkpoints step (skill fix) AND retroactively via "Variant Map" button. No map rows = universal (all variants).
- **NEW today — `m_kt_variant_price_multipliers`** (APPLIED to DB): currency-neutral multiplier per (service_cycle, variant); variant price = median(currency) × multiplier. Mapper precedence: absolute override midpoint (HVAC hand-curated era) > median × multiplier > flat median.
