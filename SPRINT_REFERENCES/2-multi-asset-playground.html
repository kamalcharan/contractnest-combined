Multi-asset servicing — the spine

  1. A visit fans out per physical asset. Apollo's MRI PM = one task per machine, each with its own calibration form = its own evidence.
  2. The event closes only when every asset is proven. Two serials done + one machine left = still open.
  3. Placeholder assets (unknown at signing): you can assign a tech and start — but you can't close until the real asset is attached, because the per-serial form can't be invoked without it.
  4. Billing is per-asset line items in one contract invoice — MRI-01, MRI-02, MRI-03 as lines, one receipt.
  5. Open the overdue MRI Preventive Maintenance visit to see it all.
