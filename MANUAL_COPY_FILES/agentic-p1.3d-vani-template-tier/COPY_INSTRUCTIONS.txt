WHAT THIS DOES (the tier ladder from the POA):
  1. TEMPLATE MATCH (deterministic): after understanding your request, the
     composer scores your SIGNED-OFF templates against it (contract type,
     nomenclature group, name/tag keywords, duration proximity). Threshold
     5+ → the catalog scan AND the LLM block selection are SKIPPED — the
     draft is instantiated from the template's saved wizard state through
     the same assemble path (pricing, assets, evidence, events, readiness).
  2. ZERO-LLM FAST PATH: simple requests ("1 month AMC for Kamal Industries
     with monthly billing") are quick-parsed by regex/keywords — the quick
     parse is only trusted when a template ALSO matches it (double
     confirmation). On that path a repeat contract composes with NO LLM
     calls: ~85s → a few seconds.
  3. CANVAS HONESTY: "⚡ Matched your template <name>" card with reasons and
     a "Compose fresh instead" escape hatch (drops the template, continues
     via catalog scan + AI selection). Zero-LLM parse shows "Request
     understood — instant · Parsed without AI — confirmed by your template."
  4. SELF-FEEDING: after a VaNi contract is sent, the success panel offers
     "Save this as a template?" — saves a DRAFT template (name input with
     suggestion); sign it off on the Templates page and the next similar
     request hits the fast path.
  5. Template-carried evidence policy and acceptance/billing defaults fill
     intent gaps (never overriding what you actually asked for). If your
     requested duration differs >15% from the template's, quantities are
     rescaled by the engine and an info gap says so.
