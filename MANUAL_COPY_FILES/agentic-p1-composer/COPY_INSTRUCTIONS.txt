WHAT THIS DELIVERS:
  "Draft with VaNi" on /contracts/create/:type — type one sentence
  ("1 year AMC for Kamal Industries with quarterly billing") and VaNi:
    1. parses intent (real LLM call),
    2. resolves the buyer from your contacts (disambiguates if needed),
    3. deterministically shortlists your catalog blocks by activity + equipment,
    4. LLM-selects blocks and FLAGS GAPS,
    5. opens the ContractWizard fully pre-filled for your review.
  Nothing is created or sent without your review. Both LLM calls are logged
  to vn_interaction_log; sending a VaNi draft reports was_accepted/was_edited.
