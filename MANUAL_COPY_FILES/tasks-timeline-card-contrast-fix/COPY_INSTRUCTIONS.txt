WHAT THIS FIXES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────
Reported: on the Tasks (seller) / My Services (buyer) tab, event cards were
nearly invisible against the panel behind them — straining to read.

Root cause (confirmed via theme values): the outer "Full Timeline" panel in
SellerTasksTab.tsx used colors.utility.secondaryBackground as its
background — and EventCard (the card component itself) ALSO uses
colors.utility.secondaryBackground for its own background. In light theme
that resolves to the exact same #ffffff for both — literally white cards
on a white panel, zero contrast.

Checked OperationsTab.tsx (the other place EventCard renders, on the
default "Operations" view) — it does NOT have this bug; its outer
container has no background style at all, so cards contrast fine against
the page canvas directly. This was specific to SellerTasksTab's extra
wrapping panel.

Fix: the outer timeline panel now uses colors.utility.primaryBackground
(a light grey-blue, #f1f4f8 in light theme) instead of secondaryBackground
— so the white EventCards now visibly stand out against it. EventCard
itself was not touched (it's shared across many other screens).

Not committed or pushed by Claude — Phase 1 only, per your procedure.
