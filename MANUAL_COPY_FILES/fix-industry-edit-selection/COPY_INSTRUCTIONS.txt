FIX: Industry Edit Mode - Show Previously Selected Industry
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

Branch: fix-industry-edit-selection
Date: 2026-02-12

PROBLEM:
When editing a business profile, the previously selected industry was not visible.
The "Currently selected" badge only showed inside sub-segment drill-down mode.
In flat mode (no hierarchy data in DB), there was zero visual feedback for the saved selection.

FIX:
- Added an always-visible "Selected: [Industry Name]" badge at the top of IndustrySelector
- Shows in ALL modes (flat + hierarchy)
- Shows amber warning if saved industry_id doesn't match any industry in catalog
- Shows nothing if industry_id is empty (new profile)
