CHANGES SUMMARY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

NEW WIZARD FLOW (8 Steps - was 6):
Step 0: Choose Path (Template vs Scratch)
Step 1: Your Role (NEW) - I'm the Client / I'm the Vendor
Step 2: Counterparty - Select buyer/vendor contact
Step 3: Acceptance - Payment, Signoff, or Auto
Step 4: Details - Name, duration, currency
Step 5: Add Blocks - Service block configuration
Step 6: Billing Cycle (NEW) - Unified Cycle / Mixed Cycles
Step 7: Review & Send - Final review (placeholder)

NEW STATE FIELDS:
- role: 'client' | 'vendor' | null
- billingCycleType: 'unified' | 'mixed' | null
