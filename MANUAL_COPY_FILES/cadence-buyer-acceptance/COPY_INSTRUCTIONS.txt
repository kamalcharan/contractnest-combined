───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT THIS BATCH DOES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2b — BUYER PICKS THE PAYMENT PLAN (public review page)
  - Every cadence-priced block shows a "Choose your payment plan"
    card above the contract document: the enabled cadences from the
    seller's rate card that fit the term, each with rate, payment
    count (+ final when the term doesn't divide) and term total.
    The seller's proposal is preselected and tagged PROPOSED.
  - Picking another plan LIVE-UPDATES the on-screen contract document
    (Schedule B rates + Schedule C payment schedule) and shows
    "With your plan the contract total is ₹X (proposed: ₹Y)".
  - Shown only while the contract is pending_acceptance and only when
    the block genuinely has ≥2 fitting options.
  - Accept sends ONLY {block_id, cycle} picks that differ from the
    proposal — never any amounts.

2c — ACCEPTANCE-TIME RESOLUTION (API + edge)
  - API /api/contracts/public/respond forwards cadence_selections
    (shape-validated) to the contracts edge function.
  - Edge (new module cadence-acceptance.ts): recomputes EVERYTHING
    from the STORED rate card — client amounts are never read:
      · validates CNAK+secret, unresponded grant, pending_acceptance
      · new rate = seller's per-cadence override ?? card rate
      · seller's hand-set final applies ONLY to the proposed cadence;
        a switched cadence uses the standard remainder suggestion
      · tax backed out of stored totals (total_price ÷ pre-tax term)
      · block row updated (billing_cycle, unit_price, total_price,
        custom_fields.config: cadenceSelectedByBuyer /
        cadenceProposedBySeller audit trail)
      · contract totals updated by delta (total_value / tax_total /
        grand_total) and the block's BILLING events regenerated inside
        computed_events (service events untouched)
      · THEN the existing respond_to_contract RPC runs — the existing
        activation trigger materializes the corrected events and
        generate_contract_invoices reads the corrected totals.
        No SQL was touched.
  - A failed reprice BLOCKS the acceptance (400) — accepting at the
    wrong price is worse than not accepting.

KNOWN LIMITS (MVP, by design)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Sign-off-link acceptance only; auto/payment acceptance keep the
  seller's proposal (locked decision).
- Seller's per-event date overrides on the changed block's BILLING
  events are regenerated on the standard schedule (service events
  and other blocks keep their dates).
- Block updates + RPC are separate statements: if the RPC fails
  after repricing, the contract stays pending with the buyer's plan
  persisted — a retry accept is consistent; a buyer who abandons
  leaves the pending contract at their picked plan.
- Contract-level percent discounts are not re-derived (no discount
  metadata is persisted today); totals move by exact block deltas.
