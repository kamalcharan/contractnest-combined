This is a real, connected playground

  1. It opens in the ghost-town state from the live data: events overdue, nothing serviced, invoices out anyway.
  2. Service a visit — from the dashboard queue or a contract's Service tab. Assign → fill the mandatory form (the definition of done) → attach a photo → Complete.
  3. Watch the KPIs move live — tickets & evidence go from 0 up, overdue drops, and a completed job drafts its invoice.
  4. Create a contract (+ New) — it generates a real schedule you then service.
  5. Everything is connected: actions in one place change the numbers everywhere.
