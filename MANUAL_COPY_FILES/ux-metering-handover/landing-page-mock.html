FAQ · what answer engines read

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED PLAINLY.

What is ContractNest?
ContractNest is a service-contract operations platform. It turns AMCs, vendor agreements, maintenance contracts, and service packages into digital contracts with e-signatures, automatic SLA tracking, evidence-backed service visits, and auto-invoicing — for both the business selling the service and the one buying it.
How much does ContractNest cost?
Plans are bundles of contract credits, not per-user licences. Free: 3 contracts. Quarterly: 90 contracts at ₹5,999/quarter (founder pricing; ₹7,999 after beta). Annual: 500 contracts at ₹18,999/year (founder pricing; ₹27,999 after). Extra contracts are ₹50 each. The VaNi AI ops assistant is a ₹5,000/month add-on during beta.
Which industries use ContractNest?
Healthcare and hospitals (equipment AMC, calibration, OT compliance), AMC and maintenance companies, wellness and fitness studios, HVAC and facility management, OEM and manufacturing field service, pest control, CA and consulting firms, real estate facilities, and IT support services.
Can I try it before creating an account?
Yes — the playground on this page builds a real contract from your industry's master data (equipment, checkpoints, service cycles, reference pricing) with no signup. If you want to keep the contract, a free account takes one minute and your draft carries over.
I buy services rather than sell them — is this for me?
Yes. The cost side gives you vendor portals, SLA clocks on every visit, work evidence required before payment, and AP tracking. Seller-side beta opens first; buyer-side is next on the roadmap.
