What this prototype demonstrates

  1. One spine, two skins — toggle Contract ⇄ Catalog Block up top: same shell, stepper, autosave, and review; only the step registry changes.
  2. Review-first templates — pick "Start from a template" and you land on a pre-filled review, not step 1. Edit only what differs.
  3. Never a silent wall — Continue is never greyed out. Press it with missing fields: inline errors, scroll-to-field, and a hint above the island.
  4. Autosave from the first keystroke — watch the pill top-right. Refresh the page mid-wizard: your draft is restored.
  5. Edit-from-review — every review card has Edit; it deep-links to the step and returns you to review on save.
  6. Phases, not 12 dots — the contract flow reads as 4 chapters (Setup · Terms · Scope · Review).
  7. Honest AI — "Draft with VaNi" fills the form and is labelled as simulated. Nothing fakes a backend.
