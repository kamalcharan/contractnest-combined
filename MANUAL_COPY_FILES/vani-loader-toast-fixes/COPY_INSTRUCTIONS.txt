WHY TOASTS WEREN'T SHOWING BEFORE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The <Toaster /> component from react-hot-toast was COMMENTED OUT in App.tsx (line 285).
This means any code using `toast.success/error()` from react-hot-toast had nowhere to render.

The useTenantProfile.ts hook was still using react-hot-toast for all its toast notifications,
which is why toasts weren't appearing even though VaNiLoader was working.

SOLUTION: Convert useTenantProfile.ts to use vaniToast (which renders via VaNiToastProviderWithGlobal)
