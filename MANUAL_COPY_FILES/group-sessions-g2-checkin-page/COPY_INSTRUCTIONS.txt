WHAT THIS DOES
----------------------------------------------------------------
Rebuilds the public QR check-in page (/checkin/:token) into a polished,
mobile-first experience (Option A: fixed branded skeleton + tenant Smart
Form content):
  - Branded header (chapter name) + "today's session" hero.
  - Step 1: big mobile-number entry (remembers device).
  - Step 2 (recognised member): welcome + past check-in count; prominent
    Present / Apologies control; the tenant's check-in Smart Form questions
    (from gs_checkin_form) rendered inline in the mobile style; your open
    dues with a pay-declaration + UPI reference; one "Check in" CTA.
  - Guests (number not on roster): name -> "Continue as guest" -> same
    attendance + form step (no dues). [Full guest tag + substitute capture
    come in G3.]
  - Polished success screen.

The Smart Form answers are now SAVED: submit sends responses +
form_template_id + form_template_version, which gs_submit_checkin persists
to t_session_attendance (form_responses / form_template_id / _version).

The form body is rendered by a small, self-contained renderer inside the
page (NOT the admin FormRenderer) on purpose — this page loads for
logged-out members on a phone and must stay dependency-light (no
ThemeContext / admin components). Field types supported: heading,
paragraph, divider, text, email, tel/phone, number, date, textarea, radio,
select, checkboxes/multiselect, boolean/checkbox/toggle, plus `required`
validation and simple conditional visibility.
