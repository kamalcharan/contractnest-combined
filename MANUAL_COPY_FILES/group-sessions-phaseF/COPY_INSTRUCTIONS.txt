WHERE THINGS ARE (answers to your questions)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Attendance beyond QR:  Group → click a session → the Occurrence screen →
  "Mark" / "Present" per member (chair marks manually, no QR).
- Dues collected marked:  Member page → Payments → "Mark collected";
  OR Overview → "Payments to confirm" → Confirm (for UPI declared at check-in).
- Session statistics:     the stats strip on the Occurrence screen.
- Smart Forms — WHERE/HOW:
    WHERE: on the member check-in page (the QR/link target, /checkin/<token>).
           When a member scans/opens it they get: identify by phone → the
           form's questions (attendance + any custom questions) → dues → submit.
    HOW configured: Smart Forms admin → author a template (status=approved) →
           add a m_form_template_mappings row for the group-session owner
           contract with timing='during_service'. If none is mapped, a built-in
           attendance-only form is shown. So every session already has a working
           form; mapping a template customises the questions.
    TEST it: QR card → "Open" (or copy the link) → that page IS the form.
           First add today's date (sessions card "Add") so it's not
           "no session today".
- Admin/chair procedure (end to end):
    1) Group page → "Generate schedule" (from the block cadence).
    2) QR card → Download/print the QR, or Copy the link to share.
    3) On session day: members self-check-in via QR/link, OR chair opens the
       occurrence and marks attendance.
    4) Dues: confirm declared UPI payments (overview) or "Mark collected"
       on the member page.
