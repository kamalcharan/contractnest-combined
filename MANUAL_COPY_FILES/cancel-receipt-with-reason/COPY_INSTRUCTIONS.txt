WHAT IT DOES:
Cancelling a receipt reverses whatever it settled: deletes its
t_invoice_receipt_allocations rows, un-settles the contract_events
those allocations touched (back to 'scheduled', or 'partial_payment'
if only part of the event was covered by this receipt), deactivates
the receipt with the captured reason + timestamp + who cancelled it,
and recomputes the parent invoice's amount_paid/balance/status.
Whole-receipt only (no partial cancel). Works regardless of the
event's cadence (monthly/quarterly/etc).
