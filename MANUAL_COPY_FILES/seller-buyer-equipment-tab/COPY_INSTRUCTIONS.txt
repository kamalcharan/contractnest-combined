SELLER + BUYER EQUIPMENT TAB — Full-Stack Implementation
===============================================================

Feature: Both seller and buyer can add/remove equipment from
         the contract detail page. Previously only the wizard
         (creation time) supported equipment addition.

Changes:
  - Seller gets "Add Equipment" button on detail page
  - Seller can remove their own equipment from detail page
  - Buyer gets "Add My Equipment" button (when allowed)
  - Buyer can remove their own equipment
  - Unified AssetPicker component for both roles
  - New seller_add/remove RPCs (migration 057)

Submodules Affected: contractnest-ui, contractnest-api, contractnest-edge
