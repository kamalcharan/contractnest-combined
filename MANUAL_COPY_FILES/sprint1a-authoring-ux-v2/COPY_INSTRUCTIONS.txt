⚠️  WHY THIS BATCH EXISTS
The previous sprint1a-authoring-ux batch shipped Coverage & Assets
logic (unit steppers, placeholders) on top of the file's EXISTING
visual layout — so on screen it looked like "old UX + a couple of
numbers," not a new UX. This batch replaces the file's presentation
layer to match the approved mock. Business logic (placeholders, unit
counts, reconciliation) is UNCHANGED from the previous batch — only
what you SEE and CLICK is different.

WHAT ACTUALLY CHANGES ON SCREEN (vs. what you tested before)
 1. No more accordion / hidden sub-categories. Every equipment type is
    a flat, always-visible card up front. Nothing is collapsed behind
    a click to "expand a category" — you see all choices immediately.
 2. Picking a type shows its ×N unit stepper INLINE on that same card
    (not a separate row/section).
 3. Once you've picked at least one type, a "Continue to attachment
    options →" button appears. Clicking it moves you to a SECOND
    stage (still inside this one wizard step — no page change): "How
    should these get attached?"
 4. That second stage shows exactly 3 stacked cards, in this order:
      a) "<Client> lists them after signing" — now the TOP, visually
         elevated (Recommended badge, bigger card) option, with
         "✓ You can still send the contract today" underneath it.
      b) "Pick from their registered <equipment/asset type>"
      c) "Register new now"
    (Previously "buyer will add" and "attach later" were two separate,
    same-weight options lower down the page — they are now ONE merged
    option, per your call to merge them, and it's promoted to the top.)
 5. Choosing "Pick from their registered…" opens a focused registry
    picker (search + list) scoped ONLY to the types you picked in
    stage 1 — not the full asset registry. Once you're done picking,
    "Done — collapse list" shrinks it to a one-line summary so the
    screen doesn't stay cluttered.
 6. An "Edit equipment types" link at the top of stage 2 takes you
    back to stage 1 without losing your selections.
 7. Right-side summary panel is lighter: just your chosen coverage
    types with counts, and (in stage 2) an attachment summary. It's
    hidden on narrower screens instead of squeezing the main content.

WHAT DOES NOT CHANGE (verify these still work exactly as before)
 - Unit-count stepper math and the "N units to attach" placeholder
   logic (placeholders auto-appear/reconcile exactly as tested in the
   prior batch).
 - Registering a brand-new asset still opens the same
   EquipmentFormDialog and auto-attaches it on success.
 - Step-level Continue/gating behavior (blocked-hint pill, "select at
   least one coverage type") is untouched — this batch only changes
   what's rendered INSIDE the step, not the wizard shell around it.
 - Parity harness / discount math on Billing View — untouched,
   verified still green (see STEP 2 below).
