WHAT'S NEW (visible changes this time — unlike Phase 1.5)
  1. PHASE STEPPER (header): the 12 dots become 4 labelled phases —
     Setup · Terms · Services · Finalize — with per-step dots.
     Every VISITED step is clickable, forward or backward. Locked
     future steps show a lock and a tooltip.
  2. CONTINUE IS NEVER SILENTLY DISABLED: pressing Continue on an
     incomplete step shows a red reason pill above the action bar
     ("Add at least one service block", "Select a counterparty…").
     Hint clears the moment you edit anything.
  3. EDIT-FROM-REVIEW: on the Review step, click any earlier step in
     the stepper → fix it → Continue returns you STRAIGHT to Review
     (no re-walking). Manual Back cancels the shortcut.
  4. AUTOSAVE HARDENED: once a draft record exists, every edit
     autosaves after 2.5s of quiet (not only on Continue). Draft
     creation threshold unchanged (past Details, needs a name).
  5. UNLOAD GUARD: closing the tab/refreshing mid-wizard with content
     now asks for confirmation (in-app Close already had a dialog).
  6. ONE PROGRESS MODEL: the bottom bar no longer carries dots — it is
     Back · step label · draft status · total · Continue.
