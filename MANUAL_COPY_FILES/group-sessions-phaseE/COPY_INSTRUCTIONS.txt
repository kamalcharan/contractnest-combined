WHAT THIS IS (the reference IA, mapped to our block-centric data)
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Overview  → block cards (each = a group session): Members / Attendance% /
              Held, Next, QR pill. + "Payments to confirm".
  Group     → click a card: KPIs (Members · Attendance% · Next · Dues due),
              "Upcoming & past sessions" (click a row → occurrence),
              "Roster" (click a member → member), "Session series", and an
              ALWAYS-VISIBLE QR card (download PNG/SVG · Open · Regenerate).
  Occurrence→ click a session: the attendance screen — mark each member
              Present/absent (chair, no QR needed), present count, + QR card,
              + per-occurrence Move date / Skip / Restore.
  Member    → click a member: profile + KPIs (Attendance% · Attended N/held ·
              Dues · Last seen) + attendance-history grid (✓/✕/·) + payments.

New RPCs (migration 023): gs_occurrence_attendance, gs_mark_attendance (chair
manual marking), gs_member_block (member-in-block profile). New API endpoints:
GET /occurrence/:id/attendance, POST /occurrence/:id/mark,
GET /member/:memberId/block/:blockId. QR is rendered inline by the vendored
qrcodegen (no npm install).

ANSWERS TO THE "I don't know how to…" points:
  - Open a specific event  → Overview → a card → click a row under
    "Upcoming & past sessions".
  - Event metrics          → that occurrence screen shows present count +
    per-member present/absent; the Group page shows attendance %.
  - Download the QR        → the QR card on the Group (and Occurrence) page —
    Download PNG/SVG, always visible.
  - Test the smart form    → QR card → "Open" (or scan) opens the member
    check-in page = the smart form. NOTE: today's date needs a scheduled
    occurrence — use "Add" on the sessions card to add today, else it says
    "no session today".

                     toasts; per-view loading + empty states
