MANUAL COPY INSTRUCTIONS - checkin-ist-timezone-fix
===============================================================

CRITICAL FIX — ALREADY LIVE. Found and fixed live during BBB's
go-live: every check-in/attendance/dashboard RPC computed "today"
from bare current_date (the DATABASE's UTC timezone), not the
tenant's local (IST) day. Between IST midnight and 5:30 AM, the
database's current_date is still YESTERDAY, so gs_resolve_checkin
(and everything else in the check-in surface) would incorrectly say
"no session today" even when it already was today in India and a
real, checkin-able session existed.
