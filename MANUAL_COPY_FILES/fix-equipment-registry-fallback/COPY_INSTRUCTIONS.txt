FIX: Equipment Registry fallback to tenant's own industry
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM:
Equipment Registry (resource-templates endpoint) returns empty when
t_tenant_served_industries has no rows, even though the tenant has
industry_id set in t_tenant_profiles (e.g. "general_practice").

FIX:
When t_tenant_served_industries is empty, fall back to
t_tenant_profiles.industry_id for the current tenant.
No new tables, no new columns, no refactoring.
