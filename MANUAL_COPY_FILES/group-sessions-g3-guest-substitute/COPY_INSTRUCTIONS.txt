WHAT THIS DOES
----------------------------------------------------------------
When a scanned mobile number is NOT on the roster, the check-in page now
asks "How are you joining today?" with two paths:

GUEST
  - Capture name (required) + optional company/email.
  - Saved as a real contact tagged 'guest' (t_contacts, tags[].tag_value=
    'guest'); that guest is marked present for today's session.
  - Re-scanning the same number reuses the existing guest contact (dedupe).

SUBSTITUTE ("standing in for a member")
  - Enter the MEMBER'S mobile -> looked up on the roster (no roster list is
    exposed) -> confirms "Standing in for {member}".
  - Enter the substitute's name.
  - The substitute is saved as the member's ALTERNATIVE CONTACT PERSON
    (a 'contact_person' child row under the member, tagged 'substitute').
  - The MEMBER is marked present (seat filled via substitute); the
    attendance row records the substitute name + is_substitute flag in
    form_responses. Attendance/streak stays on the member (your choice).
  - Re-scanning reuses the existing alternative contact (dedupe).
