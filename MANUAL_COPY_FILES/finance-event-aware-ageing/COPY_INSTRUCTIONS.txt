BATCH: finance-event-aware-ageing (2026-07-22)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
Owner decision: "invoice is 1, receipts are multiple" — the AR model is ONE
invoice per contract + the event-level settlement sub-ledger
(bbb-foundation/006). This batch makes /ops/finance statistics read from that
sub-ledger, and fixes the invoice document issues found on INV-10020.

───────────────────
1. contractnest-edge/supabase/migrations/operations-loop/020_event_aware_receivables.sql

     or edge-function change needed for it):
     * Overdue / Ageing buckets / Due-in-7-15-30 now computed from billing
       events (t_contract_events: amount - amount_settled, aged by each
       event's own scheduled_date), instead of the invoice's single due_date.
     * FIFO fallback: payments recorded without event allocations are applied

       truth per 006).
