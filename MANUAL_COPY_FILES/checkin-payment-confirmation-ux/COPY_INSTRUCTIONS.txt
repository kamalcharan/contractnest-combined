BATCH: checkin-payment-confirmation-ux (2026-07-22)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
UPI deep links ("Pay ₹X now") have no callback into the browser — once the
user's UPI app opens, this page has no way of knowing whether they paid, or
whether they even came back. Left as-is, a member can easily assume the
system detected the payment automatically and never enters the UPI
reference + taps "Record payment" — the payment then never gets declared,
and the chair never sees it to confirm.

Two things now set that expectation instead of relying on the user to
intuit it:
  1. Tapping "Pay ₹X now" opens a confirm modal first ("Before you pay —
     this isn't recorded automatically, come back and enter your reference
     + tap Record payment") with a "Continue to pay" button that actually
     opens the UPI app.
  2. If the tab regains focus after that (visibilitychange) and the
     reference still hasn't been entered, a small "Back from paying?" nudge
     appears above the UPI reference field, reminding them to finish the
     declaration. Clears itself once they start typing a reference.

No backend/data-model change — this is UI-only, same submit/declare flow as
before (member self-declares, chair confirms offline).
