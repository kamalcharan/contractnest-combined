WHAT CHANGED (per your last two messages)
────────────────────
"Group Sessions will have one consolidated list. Current [detail view]
will move to contract view as a tab."

1. Group Sessions dashboard → Roster is now a SHEET, not a click-to-
   drill-in list. One table: Member, Attended, Missed, Subbed, Status
   (OK / ⚠ over cap), Dues, and a "View contract →" link per row. A
   chair sees everyone's standing in one glance — no more opening 10
   people one at a time.

2. The per-member detail view (the single-view layout from your
   screenshot: KPI strip, policy comparison, attendance history,
   payments) has been RELOCATED — not duplicated — to a new "Sessions"
   tab on that member's own Contract detail page
   (contractnest-ui/src/components/contracts/GroupSessionTab.tsx),
   following the exact same pattern already used for the Equipment tab
   (a tab that only appears when the contract actually has a matching
   block — here, config.audience='group'). Same data, same
   useMemberBlock hook, just a different entry point: a buyer looking
   at their own contract, or a seller looking at a specific member's
   contract, instead of only being reachable from inside /group-sessions.

3. GroupSessionTab.tsx is a direct port of the ORIGINAL per-member
   screen: the same 6 separate KPI cards, the same policy strip, the
   same present/absent/substitute history grid (✓ / ✕ / S) — same
   design you already saw and liked, just relocated into the Contract
   page instead of redesigned.

3b. FOLLOW-UP FIX: dropped the "Payments (BAU dues)" card from this
    tab. Now that the view lives inside the Contract page, that card
    duplicated the Contract's own Financials tab (which already has a
    full payment/dues view with a Collect Payment CTA) — redundant
    once you're one click away from the real thing. Sessions now shows
    attendance-only data; dues/payments stay solely on Financials.

4. "View contract" on a roster row navigates to
   /contracts/:contractId?tab=sessions and lands directly on the new
   tab — added minimal tab-deep-linking support to the Contract page
   (it had none before; activeTab was always just local state
   defaulting to 'operations').

WHY THIS IS SAFE / MINIMAL
────────────────────
- No DB changes — both places read the same already-live gs_dash_roster
  and gs_member_block RPCs from the roster-stats batch.
- Equipment tab precedent followed exactly: same TabId pattern, same
  conditional-visibility approach (checks contract.blocks for a
  matching block instead of always showing), same switch-case render.
- The old /group-sessions Member screen is fully removed (not kept as
  a second copy) — one canonical place for the detail view.
