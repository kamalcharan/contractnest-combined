WHAT THIS DELIVERS (from AUDIT-WIZARD-STEPS-VS-COMPOSER.md):
  1. NOMENCLATURE MAPPING (keystone): VaNi picks from your real cat_contract_nomenclature
     master (21 items, 4 groups) as a closed choice, validated deterministically →
     nomenclatureId/Name/Group prefilled. Groups drive the Assets step + industry
     awareness (healthcare → service_delivery, no asset step).
  2. ASSETS & COVERAGE: for equipment/facility contracts VaNi reads the client's
     registered assets, prefills equipmentDetails + coverage types (derived from
     assets + selected blocks' equipment), enables 'buyer can add' + warns when
     the client has none.
  3. ACCEPTANCE: extracted from intent when stated; else defaults to Sign-off
     with an explicit info note. No more silent hard-block.
  4. BUYER NOT FOUND: VaNi drafts anyway — you select or quick-create the buyer
     in the wizard. Disambiguation gets a "none of these" option too.
  5. EVIDENCE PROPOSAL: blocks carrying form templates → smart_form with those
     forms; equipment/facility else → upload; service else → none.
  6. START DATE + GRACE parsed from intent ("from 1st August", "15 days grace").
  7. READINESS CARD: result shows "Ready: N of M steps · Needs you: …" — every
     gated wizard step arrives satisfied or explicitly flagged.
  8. GATING (business model): "Draft with VaNi" appears ONLY in Revenue mode
     (client contracts) AND for VaNi-entitled tenants. Server-side 403 on the
     composer routes for non-entitled tenants. NEW: hub (/contracts) entry button.
